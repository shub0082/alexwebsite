ABOUT US

Silver Pegasus is a blank check company incorporated as a Cayman Islands exempted company and formed for the purpose of effecting a merger, amalgamation, share exchange, asset acquisition, share purchase, reorganization or similar business combination with one or more businesses.

We may pursue an initial business combination target in any business or industry or at any stage of its corporate evolution. Our primary focus, however, will be in completing a business combination with an established business in the technology sector, with a focus on semiconductors and systems solutions.

Our sponsor, SilverLode Capital LLC, is a Delaware limited liability company, which was formed to invest in us.

Please refer to the Registration Statement for additional information.
